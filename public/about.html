    Where did this come from?

    Snow Project's conception is the fault of one man named Caleb Madrigal. Time and again he had played with the idea in his head, musing over its legitimacy as a business and likelihood of succeeding if executed. Eventually he came to rest the responsibility of its creation upon the shoulders of his brother-in-law Jared Sasser (me :D). Serving as a tutor of sorts to Jared thereafter, Caleb came up with a basic layout for the website and thus Snow Project was born at last and could finally bring joy to all the boys and girls around the world (or something like that; I ran out of things to say at this point).

    But Snow Project still isn't an actual business...

    Oh... you noticed that did you?... Well the thing is, this project's actual main purpose was to give me practice in web design and development. I was in need of an idea for a (somewhat) professional website to work on and Caleb just happened to have this on the brain at the time of my situation. So while it may not be actually out there spreading happiness to all mankind, rest assured that it has served (is serving) a worthy cause. Don't worry about it though; I'm certain some Joe Schmoe out there will take advantage of the demand for this service in the world and take appropriate action.
